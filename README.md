# Gas Já

Gás Já! is a web platform designed to connect customers with local gas suppliers, making it easier to find nearby gas providers and request deliveries quickly.

The idea for this project came from a real-world need identified by a gas supplier, who noticed that many customers still rely on phone calls or manual searches to find available gas deliveries.

The goal of Gas Já is to centralize local suppliers in a single platform, following a marketplace model similar to services like food delivery apps.

---

## How it works

### Customer
- Creates an account
- Provides their address
- Views nearby gas suppliers
- Places gas delivery orders

### Supplier
- Creates an account
- Registers their gas depot
- Receives customer orders
- Accepts or rejects delivery requests
